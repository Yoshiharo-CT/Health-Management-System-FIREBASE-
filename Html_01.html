Quick overview of your Health Management System

Viewing Patient Records

  • Open the Patient Dashboard from the sidebar.
  • See all registered patients in the table.
  • Columns displayed:
    • First & Last Name: Patient identity
    • Email: Primary contact method
    • Date Recorded: Timestamp of when the patient was added

Adding a New Patient

  • Locate the input fields at the top left: First Name, Last Name, Email.
  • Enter the patient's details in the boxes.
  • Click the Add Patient button.
  • The patient list updates automatically with the new entry.

Searching for a Patient

  • Type the patient's name or search criteria into the Search Patient box.
  • Click the Find button.
  • The table filters to show only matching results.
  • If no match is found, a message "No patient found" will appear.

Resetting the View

  • Click the Reset button to clear search filters.
  • The full list of patients will be displayed again.

Appointments

  • View your scheduled appointments on the Appointment page.
  • Check the date, time, and assigned doctor for each visit.
  • Patients can track upcoming visits easily through the dashboard.
  • Staff and Admin: You can also add new appointments for patients here.

Doctors Directory

  • See the list of available doctors in the Doctor Dashboard.
  • Patients can check doctor names, specialties, and contact info for appointments.
  • Staff and Admin: Add, update, or remove doctor profiles as needed.
  • Helps you choose the right doctor for your needs.

System Settings

  • Go to the Settings page from the sidebar.
  • Edit profile information, update contact details, or manage preferences.
  • Logout securely using the Logout button.
